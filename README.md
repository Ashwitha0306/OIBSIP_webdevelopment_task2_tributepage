The Pioneers of Computing – Digital Museum

An interactive and visually immersive digital museum that celebrates the pioneers who shaped modern computing. This project showcases the lives, achievements, innovations, and lasting impact of legendary figures such as Alan Turing, Ada Lovelace, Grace Hopper, Steve Jobs, and Tim Berners-Lee through engaging timelines, galleries, fun facts, quotes, and an AI-powered chat experience.
